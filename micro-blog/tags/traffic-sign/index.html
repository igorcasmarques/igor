Say quoi 4 (Family Day 2024)

February 19, 2024
Igor Coelho A. S. Marques
Technical writer in Ottawa, Canada

Remember that grotesque traffic sign from Québec that said « Attention à nos enfants » and depicted a hurt child lying on the ground? Compare it to this one by the City of Ottawa:

  • Same message → "slow down"
  • Same concept → text + picture of children
  • Different execution → the children are alive (and having fun)

Which one is more effective in making drivers slow down and preventing accidents, I wonder ... and if it's the panneau québecois, does the end justify the means?
